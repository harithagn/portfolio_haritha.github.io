Project2:

Title:Hybrid Metaheuristic optimization algorithm to address localization problem

Duration:6 months

Project Overview:

Our groundbreaking hybrid metaheuristic algorithm presents an innovative solution to the localization challenge in Wireless Sensor Networks (WSNs). By combining the strengths of two powerful optimization algorithms, the Bat Algorithm (BA) and Grey Wolf Optimization Algorithm (GWO), our approach achieves unparalleled performance in localizing sensor nodes within the network.
